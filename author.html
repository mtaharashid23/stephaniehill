About Stephanie

Thyroid Cancer Survivor.
Storyteller. Advocate.

"Even in the darkest moments, healing and meaning are still possible."

Stephanie is a thyroid cancer survivor, writer, and advocate for women navigating the intersection of illness, grief, and recovery. Her journey, captured in Healing Tears, began with years of overlooked symptoms and medical dismissal, culminating in a life-threatening thyroid storm, a Stage IV cancer diagnosis, and an eight-hour surgery that changed everything.

On the same day she fought for her life, Stephanie lost her twin brother her closest bond and lifelong source of strength. That loss reshaped her healing, turning survival into something deeper than physical recovery. It became a journey of rebuilding identity, trust, and purpose in the aftermath of profound trauma.

Through her writing, Stephanie offers an unfiltered look at what it truly means to survive not just the treatments and procedures, but the silent battles that follow. She speaks to the loneliness, the emotional weight, and the resilience required to keep going when life no longer feels familiar.

Today, she uses her voice to help others feel seen, understood, and less alone. Her work is rooted in honesty, compassion, and the belief that even in the darkest moments, healing and meaning are still possible.
