The Book

More Than a Survival Story

Healing Tears is a powerful and deeply personal journey through illness, loss, and the long road back to "whole," the same, yet different "whole." In this memoir, Stephanie shares her experience of surviving Stage IV thyroid cancer while navigating unimaginable emotional pain, including the loss of her twin brother at the most critical moment of her life.

This compelling story offers guidance, inspiration, strength, and reassurance to anyone facing hardship. It reminds readers that even in the darkest moments, resilience can be rebuilt, purpose can be rediscovered, and life, though changed, can still be meaningful and whole.
