The Book

More Than a Survival Story

Healing Tears is a powerful and deeply personal journey through illness, loss, and the long road back to life. In this memoir, Stephanie shares her experience of surviving thyroid cancer while navigating unimaginable emotional pain, including the loss of her twin brother at the most critical moment of her life.

Her story offers guidance, strength, and reassurance to anyone facing hardship. It reminds readers that even in the darkest moments, resilience can be rebuilt, purpose can be rediscovered, and life though changed can still be meaningful and whole.

About the Author

Stephanie Hill

She didn't set out to write a book. She set out to survive.

Stephanie Hill is a cancer survivor, a grieving sister, and a woman who chose to turn her most painful years into something that might help someone else feel less alone.

She lives and writes with the belief that honesty — even when it hurts — is where healing begins.

If you have ever felt like you were losing yourself — this book was written for you.
